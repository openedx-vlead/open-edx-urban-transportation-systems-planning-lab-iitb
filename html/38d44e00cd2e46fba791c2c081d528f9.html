FAQs
1. Is System optimization a realistic model? If not, why we use it to model flow in a network?
System optimization is not a realistic model but it is very useful to transport planners and engineers, trying to manage the traffic to minimise travel costs and therefore achieve an optimum social equilibrium.

2.Can User equilibrium and system optimal produce identical results?
When congestion effects are ignored (i.e. travel time is independent of flow) , User equilibrium and system optimal have same optimization function and hence produce identical results.
